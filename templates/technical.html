Setting Camera and Microphone on different browser
Google Chrome
If you are using Google Chrome, please follow the steps below. This is also available on Google's support documentation here:User your camera and microphone
1. Open Chrome Chrome

2. At the top right, click More More and then Settings.

3. At the bottom, click Advanced.

4. Under "Privacy and security," click Content settings.

5. Click Camera or Microphone.

6. Turn Ask before accessing on or off.
Microsoft Edge
To access your browser camera permissions: If you'd like to access all permissions, please follow Microsoft's documentation here:Windows 10 camera, microphone, and privacy
1. Go to the website that wants to use your microphone and/or camera.

2. If a dialog box appears asking if you want to give the website permission to use your camera or microphone, select Allow once or Always allow, or close the dialog box to block access.
